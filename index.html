About

Dive into the future of AI with us at the "Advanced Summer School on Responsible AutoML"! This event is your gateway to the cutting-edge world of Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), all through the lens of ethical AI innovation. It's the perfect spot for researchers, students, and professionals alike to deepen their understanding, connect with like-minded individuals, and work together towards the responsible development of AI. Take this opportunity to improve your knowledge and skills in AutoML. Let's embark on this adventure together!

What you'll learn

  • Measure and improve model fairness
  • Build interpretable AutoML pipelines
  • Mitigate risk with safe deployment
  • Apply concepts in guided labs

Who should attend

  • Students (undergrad and graduate) in CS/DS
  • ML/AI researchers and practitioners
  • Data scientists and ML engineers
  • Anyone implementing trustworthy AI in practice
